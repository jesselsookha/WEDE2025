The Role of Semantic HTML

Semantic HTML elements clearly describe their meaning both to the browser and the developer. For example, <article> defines independent, self-contained content.

"Semantic HTML is the foundation of accessible and well-structured content."

— Web Standards Advocate

Did you know?

The <aside> element is used for tangential content, like notes or related links.
